Schedules

See our PVTA buses, maps, service calendar, and trip planner pages for more information about our routes and service area. You can also view schedules on your mobile phone at m.pvta.com.

Printed schedules and maps are available on our buses, at the UMass bus garage, and at the UMass Campus Center concourse information booth. The following schedules go into effect September 6, 2011. Through September 4th, 2011, note that Routes 34, 35, 38, and 39 are not running. Routes 30, 31, 45, and 46 are running reduced service. Routes 32 and 37 are running full service. There will be No Service on September 5th 2011.
