How to Advertise

  • Contact the Marketing Coordinator to discuss contract terms, pricing, availability, and so forth.
  • Sign a contract that includes costs, number of spaces, duration of display, etc.
  • Design an advertisement, making sure that it’s consistent with university guidelines and approved by the Marketing Coordinator.
  • Print the sign (either with your own contractor, or through ours) and have it delivered to the PVTA Bus Facility. We’ll install it

Pricing

  • Cost of each sign printed varies, but is usually around $175.00. Your printer can give you an exact cost.
  • Each spaces costs $125.00 per month.
  • Semester discount: for each 4-month semester purchased, we reduce the cost by 1 month.
  • On-campus discount: On-campus groups (departments, RSO’s, and so forth) are eligible for a 50% discount.
  • Total price will include any printing costs, space costs, and any miscellaneous costs.
  • Payment will be via university recharge unless otherwise arranged.
