Campus Service

Main
Does your Registered Student Organization or University department need transportation for a large group? Call on UMass Transit's Field Trip Service! Whether you want to go to Boston for the day, or to Sunderland for the afternoon, we can get you there.

Contact the Field Trip Service at least two weeks before your trip to schedule a trip with us. If Field Trip Services doesn't fit your needs, we can also give you information on contacting other transportation vendors.

Contact the Field Trip Service

Our Vehicles

School Buses: Our buses are designed with extra leg room, and have seating capacity for 38.

UMass Maroon and White: These buses are considerably more comfortable than a school bus and have seating capacity for 38.

Vans: Our vans have extra room for luggage, and seating capacity for 10.

Wheelchair Accessible Vans: These vans have seating capacity for 2-4 with room for up to two wheelchairs.
