Meet & Greet

The Meet and Greet Service provides ground transportation to and from the Amherst campus. Destinations include Bradley International Airport ,the UMass President‘s Office and the Springfield Amtrak station. Our courteous, professionally trained drivers provide a relaxing and comfortable mode of transportation for employees and guests of the University.

RATES PER TRIP
To or From Sedan Van
Amtrak/Springfield $80 $110
Bradley Airport (Inside terminal greeting option+$5) $95 $130
Pioneer Valley Area ($50 minimum) $28/hr $38/hr
Outside Pioneer Valley ($50 minimum) $40/hr $48/hr


All payments must be made using a University Recharge. Please schedule trips at least 3 business days in advance. You can request trips by filling out the Meet and Greet Request Form.
